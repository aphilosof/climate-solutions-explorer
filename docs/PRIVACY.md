# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date**: January 14, 2026
**Last Updated**: January 14, 2026

Climate Solutions Explorer ("we," "our," or "the application") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our web application.

---

## TL;DR (Summary)

- ✅ **Anonymous, cookieless analytics only** - We count page views and feature usage via [GoatCounter](https://www.goatcounter.com/) without cookies, IP storage, or personal identification
- ✅ **No cookies** - We only use localStorage for favorites (client-side only)
- ✅ **No third-party data sharing** - Your data stays with us
- ✅ **Open source** - You can verify our practices
- ⚠️ **Form submissions** - If you submit content via our form, we collect your name, email, and submission details

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Don't Need to Provide

When you browse the Climate Solutions Explorer, **we do NOT collect**:
- IP addresses (not stored)
- Browser fingerprints
- Behavioral tracking data or session recordings
- Location data beyond country-level statistics
- Anything that identifies you personally

**Anonymous usage statistics:** we use [GoatCounter](https://www.goatcounter.com/), a privacy-friendly, open-source analytics service, to understand which parts of the Explorer are useful. It counts page views and in-app events (which visualization was opened, search terms entered into the site search, which solution panels were viewed) **without cookies and without storing personal data** — no consent banner is required under GDPR/ePrivacy. GoatCounter's own privacy policy: https://www.goatcounter.com/help/privacy

### 1.2 Information You Voluntarily Provide

#### Form Submissions (Content Contribution Form)

If you submit a climate solution via our Google Form, we collect:

**Required Information**:
- Your name
- Your email address
- Entry title
- Entry URL
- Entry type (e.g., Company, Article, etc.)
- Entry description
- Category selections
- Keywords/tags

**Optional Information**:
- Your affiliation (organization)
- Location (for Person/Company entries)
- Comments for reviewers
- Climate Drift membership status ("Driftie")

**Purpose**: To review, approve, and add your contribution to the database.

**Retention**: Submissions are stored indefinitely in our Google Sheets database for content management purposes.

**Access**: Only approved administrators (currently: [info@climatesolutionsmap.earth](mailto:info@climatesolutionsmap.earth)) can access submissions.

### 1.3 Local Storage (Client-Side Only)

We use **browser localStorage** to save your:
- **Favorite solutions**: Stored locally in your browser only
- **Search preferences**: May be cached locally for faster performance

**Important**: This data never leaves your device. It is not transmitted to our servers.

**Clearing Data**: You can clear this data anytime through your browser settings or by clicking "Clear Favorites" in the application.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

### 2.1 Form Submissions

Information collected through the submission form is used to:
- Review and validate submissions
- Contact you about your submission (approval, questions, etc.)
- Attribute content to you when published
- Maintain quality and accuracy of the database
- Send automated email notifications to administrators

### 2.2 Public Display

When your submission is approved:
- **Published**: Title, URL, description, tags, type, date
- **NOT published**: Your email address, reviewer comments, private notes
- **Optionally published**: Author/organization name (if you chose to include it)
